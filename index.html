About us

Trinity Fitness is a Christian company that combines the strength of physical fitness, the healing of physical therapy, and the guidance of Bible study, empowering individuals to grow in body, mind, and spirit.

  • All sorts of exercise machines and weights.
  • Professionally trained physcial therapists.
  • Bible studies every week to strenghten yourself spiritually.

At Trinity Fitness, we foster a supportive community where faith and wellness intertwine, helping individuals grow in body, mind, and spirit while living out God's purpose with confidence.

Day Pass

Our gym day pass gives you full-day access to all our facilities, equipment, and amenities.

Monthly Membership

Our monthly membership offers unlimited access to all gym facilities, equipment, and amenities with a convenient monthly payment.

Yearly Membership

Our yearly membership provides full access to all gym facilities, equipment, and amenities with a single annual payment, offering long-term convenience at a slightly higher price.

Premium Membership

Our premium pass grants unlimited access to all gym facilities, equipment, and classes, with a single payment every six months at a higher price for ultimate flexibility and value.
